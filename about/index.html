Design Process

1. Research and Discover

I look for similar products both in the digital and the non-digital spaces. By doing this, I know the possible expectations my clients may have.

2. Ideate

I make use of affinity mapping to organize the ideas that I collected. With this, I make a rough prototype of the product using Figma or Lunacy.

3. Deliver

I deliver insights to the client by summarizing the details of the affinity mapping I made and show them the prototype. This is necessary to see their expectation towards the product.

4. Develop

After analyzing and organizing the ideas from the kickoff meeting, I start developing the product. I base the development from a refined version of the prototype I made.

5. Test and Repeat

I let clients and their possible customers test the product to see what should be improved on. If there is a need for improvement, I repeat the process.
